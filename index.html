O'AHU RESTAURANT RESERVATION POLICY

Due to the increase in no-show reservations we will no longer be accepting any reservations for parties under 20. All parties of 20+ may place a reservation however, we require a $150 deposit to reserve. The $150 deposit will go toward your bill on the day of your party.

Please note that we will NOT hold your reservation until the deposit is paid. Your deposit is non-refundable if you cancel within less than 24 hours of your reservation.

Any reservations with a party of 40+ is considered a “large event” and must email brickovenpizzahawaii@gmail.com to book.

Parties of 20-30 must spend a minimum of $350 and parties of 40 must spend a minimum of $750. *This is NOT the minimum if you are renting out the restaurant for parties over 40.* Not including the 18% autogratuity.
